Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

At Lumen, your privacy is our priority. We designed this app to be a private tool for your mental wellbeing. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data, affirmations, or self-reflections.

All data created within Lumen, including custom affirmations, saved favorites, and preferences, is stored locally on your device. We do not use external servers to store this information, and it never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up via your device's native iCloud or similar backup services.

3. Telemetry and Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools within the app. Your usage remains entirely confidential.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Support page.
